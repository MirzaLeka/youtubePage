Stories We Share

Editing is an art
It's not just about puting scenes and music together. It's about coming up with a story in your mind and figuring out how are you gonna share that story with the audience in 2-3 minutes video.

It has to be perfect
Scenes must fit with the audio, you must feel every bumb you see in video and here a noise in the background and above all it has to be fun to watch.
We always aim for perfection and although we'll never achieve it, we set a bar each new video. Every new video must be better than the previous.

Alone we fight, together we dominate
While one guy is editing clips, another one is mixing audio for his video. That's how we roll.
There are so many people out there that contribute to our work. They share their opinions, they ecnourage us to go deeper and also allow us to use their voices when we need it. Whole process is a lot of fun!
