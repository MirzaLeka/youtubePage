Insurgency


Last time we made a war video was a Tribute To D-day, a year ago and we figured that the best way to go back to that genre is with Insurgency.

Insurgency is multiplayer only first person shooter series developed by New World Interactive. Series is set in modern time and features high emphasis on teamplay, immersive firefights, emphasis on realism.
One bullet and you're down.

All of those elements inspired us to innovate and start creating something we haven't done yet.

STORY


Beside puting scenes and music together we always tend to pay attention to the narrative. Since it's a multiplayer game, we tried to write our own story we experienced while playing this game for many hours.

With Insurgency we want to tell a story of soldiers that fight in this war. And there is a scene where one of the soldiers calls his wife and tells her that everything is going be ok.
It has that personal touch we want to bring forward.

TEAM


The heart of the Insurgency is teamplay. We didn't want to make a video just about player shooting dozens of enemies. We tried to capture as much team actions as possible.

Our final product we'll be all about that. We want to make you feel as a part of a group, rather than an individual.

RELEASE


Insurgency is a big project. We have our own story we want to tell, we have 100 GBs of recorder gameplay videos.
We are putting all of those elements together in to something we hope people will enjoy. So stay tuned.

Release date: Summer 2018.
