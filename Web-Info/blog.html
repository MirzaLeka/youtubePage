Insurgency


It's been a year since our last war video came out, Tribute To D-day and we figured that the best way to go back to that genre is with a modern tone.

Insurgency is multiplayer only first person shooter series developed by New World Interactive. Series is set in modern time and features high emphasis on teamplay, immersive firefights and emphasis on realism.
One bullet and you're down.

All of those things inspired us to innovate and start creating something we haven't done before.

STORY


Beside putting scenes and music together we always tend to pay attention to the narrative. Since it's a multiplayer game, we tried to write our own story we experienced while playing this game for many hours.

With Insurgency we want to tell a story of soldiers that fight in this fictional war. And there is a scene where one of the soldiers calls his wife on a phone and tells her that everything is going be ok, even though he doesn't know what's gonna happen.
It has that personal touch we want to bring forward.

COMBAT


The heart of the Insurgency is teamplay. We don't want to make a video about player shooting dozens of enemies. What we are trying to do is capture as much team actions as possible.

Our final product we'll be all about that. This is a video about group of soldiers who fight through chaos together, rather than an individual who saves the day.

RELEASE


Insurgency is a big project for us. We have a story we want to tell and we also have 100 GBs of recorder gameplay videos.
We are combining all previously mentioned elements together into something we hope you will enjoy watching. Stay tuned.

Release date: Summer 2018.
